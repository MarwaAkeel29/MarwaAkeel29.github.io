Our Legacy

Al Tayer Group is a diversified regional business established in 1979, headquartered in the United Arab Emirates. With a commitment to excellence, we have developed a reputation for delivering world-class experiences across various sectors.

Our Core Divisions

The Group operates in four key areas:Automotive, Retail, Real Estate, and Ventures. Each division is driven by innovation and a deep understanding of regional markets.

Who We Are

At Al Tayer Group, we pride ourselves on a curated portfolio of prestigious brands and a strong presence across the Middle East. With over four decades of experience, we have cultivated a business model centered around passion, trust, and long-term relationships.
Our ventures span industries such as automotive, fashion & luxury retail, hospitality, real estate, interior contracting, travel, engineering, and healthcare, making us one of the most dynamic and respected conglomerates in the region.

Driven by Excellence
